/  What to expect  /

Six stages through real internship territory.

  1. 01

    Browse & apply

    Explore real graduate roles on seek.jobs, draft your CV, and apply to companies that suit you.

  2. 02

    Resume outcome

    Application updates land in your personal inbox with feedback on what worked and what didn't. Some you'll get; some you won't.

  3. 03

    Interview

    If you're shortlisted, you'll have a real interview conversation with the hiring manager — typed, but properly back-and-forth.

  4. 04

    Work tasks

    Three progressively harder briefs from your mentor, with feedback on each. Halfway through you'll have a quick mid-placement coaching chat before tackling the last one.

  5. 05

    Lunchroom moments

    Your colleagues will invite you to informal team lunches — group chats where you get to know the team off the clock. The bit most internships forget to teach.

  6. 06

    Exit interview

    Wrap up with a short reflective conversation with HR. Not a test — a chance to think back on what you learned and what you'd do differently.
