/  What to expect  /

A four-stage journey through real internship territory.

  1. 01

    Browse & apply

    Explore real graduate roles on seek.jobs, draft your CV, and apply to companies that suit you.

  2. 02

    Wait on the outcome

    Application updates land in your personal inbox. Some you'll get; some you won't.

  3. 03

    Interview & start work

    If you're shortlisted, you'll have a real interview conversation, then begin your simulated internship.

  4. 04

    Complete the internship

    Take on tasks, work with your team, and finish the cycle with the experience to do it for real.
